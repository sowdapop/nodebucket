About Nodebucket



Are you tired of wasting page after page of note pads, sticky notes, and calendars? Does it frustrate you to no end to create those notes, tasks, and to-do lists, just to forget them at home when you need them most? Those new waste-free notebooks are great for the environment, but are easily forgotten at home, in the car, in a bag at a friend’s house. You know what is always with you? Your mobile phone! Why spend all that time and energy writing down reminders and trying to keep track of another tangible item in your life? Try tracking down a planner while ensuring a two-year-old (child or animal) stays out of trouble. Good luck! We understand the struggle of needing more than a calendar app to remind you of tasks, priorities, and due dates. That’s why we created Nodebucket.


Nodebucket is an easy-to-use task management software that allows you to create, track, edit, and delete time sensitive material. It is accessible through your computer and your mobile phone, allowing seamless transitions from work to on-the-go. Nodebucket offers drag-and-drop and tap-to-delete organization to help you prioritize tasks. Push notifications remind you of upcoming due dates and high priority items. Our software makes it easy to keep your mind clear and focused with tasks laid out right when you open the app. No getting lost here! Feeling overwhelmed with the number of tasks to complete? Hide lower priority tasks for later and focus on what is most important. Nodebucket will become your personal assistant, keeping you on track and on time.
